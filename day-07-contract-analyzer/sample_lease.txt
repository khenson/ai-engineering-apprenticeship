RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This lease is entered into between Sunset Properties LLC ("Landlord") and Tenant.

1. TERM: Lease begins January 1, 2025 and runs month-to-month with no fixed end date.

2. RENT: Monthly rent is $2,400, due on the 1st. Late fee of $200 applies after the 3rd day. Landlord may increase rent with 24 hours written notice.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT: Tenant pays $4,800 security deposit. Landlord may use deposit for any purpose deemed necessary and will return remainder within 90 days of move-out.

4. ENTRY: Landlord may enter the property at any time without prior notice for inspections, repairs, or any other reason.

5. REPAIRS: Tenant is responsible for all repairs under $500. Tenant must obtain written approval for any repair over $500, which Landlord may deny at sole discretion.

6. TERMINATION: Landlord may terminate lease with 24 hours notice. Tenant must provide 60 days written notice to terminate.

7. LIABILITY: Tenant assumes all liability for any injuries occurring on the property regardless of cause, including Landlord negligence.

8. PETS: No pets allowed. Violation results in immediate termination and forfeiture of entire security deposit.

9. SUBLETTING: No subletting permitted under any circumstances. Violation results in immediate eviction and $5,000 penalty.

10. GOVERNING LAW: This agreement is governed by the laws of the state where the property is located.
